earthMapps.io

Earth's maps for application oriented science

Remote sensing science

EarthMapps is a research team of Delft University of Technology (TUDelft) under supervision of Stef Lhermitte. We focus on the use of multi-source satellite imagery to assess land-atmosphere interactions. Our research aims at developing innovative remote sensing methods for assessing the effect of climate (change) on snow & ice, vegetation dynamics, sea level rise, etc. We combine remote sensing with a range of models (e.g., climate models, snow models, radiative transfer models) to improve our understanding of Earth's changes. We work on big data solutions to bridge the gap between remote sensing technology and its applications.
